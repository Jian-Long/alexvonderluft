Privacy

A clear summary of analytics used on this website.

Analytics

This website sends privacy-focused, aggregate usage events to Umami Cloud. Umami states that its analytics tracker uses no cookies, fingerprinting or cross-site tracking and does not collect personal data. Processing takes place in Umami Cloud's EU region.

We measure page visits, campaign attribution, coarse device category, country-level location provided by Umami, scroll milestones, Core Web Vitals and clicks on important links. We do not intentionally send names, email addresses, chat contents, passwords, tokens or arbitrary URL parameters.

The site does not store campaign attribution or a returning-visitor identifier in your browser. Analytics runs automatically without a consent cookie or advertising profile.

Purpose and retention

The purpose is to understand aggregate site performance and which campaigns lead to platform visits. The selected data region, retention period and data-processing agreement must be confirmed by the site operator in the Umami Cloud account before production use.

Limits

An Exclusive click shows only that a visitor followed a website link. It does not prove an OnlyFans subscription or payment. In-app browser detection is an estimate.
